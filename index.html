WHO ARE WE?

Ptarmigan is a custom millwork and renovation company. We specialize in custom high quality hardwood furniture and
residential space solutions. Everything we make is designed and made in Edmonton from sustainably sourced materials
and put together using timeless hand-craft techniques.

OUR WORK PLACE

Welcome to our workshop! We believe in fostering a happy and collaborative environment that inspires creativity. Our team of skilled artisans thrives on the joy of woodworking, and we’ve cultivated a space where passion and laughter are as essential as the tools we use. While creativity flourishes here, safety remains our top priority. We’ve implemented rigorous safety protocols, ensuring that every participant is equipped with the knowledge and protective gear necessary for accident-free crafting. Our workshops are designed not only to hone your woodworking skills but also to provide a supportive and uplifting community.

We care about our planet and workers

Ptarmigan takes pride in promoting resource sustainability at every step of our creative process. From the selection of materials to the crafting techniques employed, we prioritize eco-friendly practices. We source our wood from responsibly managed forests, ensuring that every piece used in our creations contributes to the long-term health of the environment. Embracing the principles of reduce, reuse, and recycle, we minimize waste by repurposing leftover materials into smaller projects or incorporating them into other designs. Additionally, our commitment extends to utilizing non-toxic finishes that are both safe for our craftsmen and environmentally friendly. By blending artistry with sustainability, we strive to create not only beautiful, one-of-a-kind pieces but also contribute to a greener and more sustainable future for our planet.
